📘 Legal Awareness Hub

Know Your Rights. Stay Safe.

🚨 Stalking

Repeatedly following or contacting someone without consent.

Punishment: Up to 3 years imprisonment + fine

💬 Sexual Harassment

Unwanted physical contact, comments, or messages.

Punishment: Up to 3 years imprisonment + fine

🌐 Cyber Crime

Online threats, hacking, or blackmail.

Punishment: Up to 3–5 years imprisonment + fine

🚫 Assault

Physical attack or use of force.

Punishment: Imprisonment + fine

🛑 Acid Attack

Throwing acid causing severe injury.

Punishment: Minimum 10 years to life imprisonment
